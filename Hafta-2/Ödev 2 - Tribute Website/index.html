Person of Interest

Person of Interest is a thrilling TV series that explores the intersection of artificial intelligence, surveillance, and morality...

Harold Finch creates a machine for the government to predict terrorist threats, but he secretly uses it to stop violent crimes...

The show raises questions about privacy, government control, and the ethical implications of AI surveillance...

Throughout the series, Finch and his team navigate dangerous situations to stop crimes while staying hidden from the authorities...
